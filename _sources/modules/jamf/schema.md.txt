### JamfComputerGroup

Representation of a Jamf computer group.

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
|id|The group id|
|name|The friendly name of the group|
|is_smart| Whether the group is [smart](https://docs.jamf.com/10.4.0/jamf-pro/administrator-guide/Smart_Computer_Groups.html)|
